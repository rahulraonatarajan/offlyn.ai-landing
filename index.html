TerraGuide Features

TerraGuide™ is a comprehensive outdoor safety and communication platform that combines on-device AI assistance, mesh networking, offline navigation, and emergency preparedness into a single, privacy-focused iOS application designed for outdoor enthusiasts and emergency situations.

On-Device AI Assistant

Meta Llama 3.2 model provides instant emergency guidance, survival tips, and medical assistance without requiring internet connectivity.

Mesh Networking

Dual-transport mesh networking combines Bluetooth and WiFi for reliable communication up to 10 miles without cell towers.

Offline Navigation

Full offline maps with terrain data, GPS navigation, and emergency waypoint management for complete navigation without internet.

Emergency Features

Emergency beacon broadcasting, SOS flashlight patterns, and automated distress signals for critical situations.

FEMA Integration

Local access to official FEMA emergency guides, disaster-specific guidance, and emergency kit checklists.

Privacy & Security

Zero-knowledge architecture ensures AI conversations never leave your device, with end-to-end encryption for all communications.

RAVIN

Reliable AI for Vital INformation

"Understand your health, privately."

RAVIN helps you understand your lab results and doctor's notes — privately, clearly, and instantly. It runs entirely offline on your device, using local AI to translate complex medical data into plain, human language.

No accounts. No uploads. Just clarity and peace of mind.

Whether it's your blood test, cholesterol check, or kidney panel, RAVIN explains what your numbers mean, what's normal, and when it might be time to talk to your doctor — without ever replacing one.

Built on a foundation of privacy, transparency, and trust, RAVIN makes medical information understandable and accessible for everyone, everywhere.

Important Medical Disclaimer

RAVIN does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. It is designed to help interpret and understand medical information for educational purposes only. Always consult with qualified healthcare professionals for medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment decisions. RAVIN should be used as a supplementary tool to enhance understanding, not as a replacement for professional medical care.
